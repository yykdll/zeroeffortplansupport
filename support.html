Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I sync my data across devices?

A: Enable iCloud sync in the app settings. Your plans and reminders will be securely synced via your Apple ID.

Q: I purchased a subscription but features are still locked. What should I do?

A: Please try restoring purchases from the subscription page. If the issue persists, contact us with your Apple ID and purchase details.

Q: How can I delete all my data?

A: You can delete your data from within the app or by uninstalling the app from your device.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes. All your data is stored locally or in your private iCloud account. We do not access or share your personal data.
